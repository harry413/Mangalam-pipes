Advanced HDPE Pipe Manufacturing Process

Our state-of-the-art extrusion technology ensures consistent quality, optimal material properties, and dimensional accuracy in every pipe we manufacture.

High-Grade Raw Material Selection

Vacuum sizing tanks ensure precise outer diameter while internal pressure maintains perfect roundness and wall thickness uniformity.

  • PE100 grade material
  • Optimal molecular weight distribution

Precision Extrusion Technology

Our advanced extruders maintain precise melt temperatures and pressures to ensure uniform pipe wall thickness and consistent material properties throughout production.

  • Twin-screw extrusion system
  • Real-time temperature monitoring

Controlled Cooling Process

Gradual cooling in water baths ensures stress-free pipe formation, preventing deformation and maintaining precise dimensions throughout the cooling zone.

  • Multi-zone water cooling
  • Temperature-controlled baths

Vacuum Sizing & Calibration

Precision vacuum sizing ensures exact outer diameter compliance with international standards while maintaining perfect circularity.

  • Laser diameter measurement
  • ±0.1mm tolerance control

Rigorous Quality Control

Every pipe undergoes comprehensive testing including hydrostatic pressure tests, dimensional checks, and material property verification.

  • 100% hydrostatic testing
  • IS 5984 / ISO 4427 compliance

Automated Pipe Marking

Ink-jet marking systems apply all required product information including size, pressure rating, standard, and batch traceability codes.

  • UV-resistant ink marking
  • Full traceability codes

Precision Cutting to Length

Automated planetary saws cut pipes to specified lengths with burr-free, square ends ready for immediate installation or coiling.

  • Automated length measurement
  • Burr-free clean cuts

Professional Packaging & Dispatch

Finished pipes are bundled, strapped, and labeled according to customer specifications and international shipping standards.

  • Custom bundle sizes
  • Export-ready packaging
